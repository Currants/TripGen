Additional Data

When files are appended, all survey files are uploaded (if any data is collected, at all). This provides an option to upload a file for:

* map for XY coordinate of location surveyd

* weather? scrap weather underground for source nearest XY - parcipitation, temperature averages for each hour surveyed

* Smart growth db? Link XY/block group ID to connect with online hosted smart growth db. Provide information about density, etc, and maybe suggestions for similar block groups? 

* Possibly link smart growth characteristics to our hosted data base... Meaning, we could suggest comparing with certain land use/BE locations in our national database?
